Development Environment/How I work

As of right now, I do all of my work on my Macbook Pro. It's only a 13 inch laptop but it serves its purpose. I'm considering buying a iMac to work on any future design projects. Having a laptop is convienent but sometimes I find my face getting closer to the screen the longer I use it. I definitely need a bigger screen!

In addition to my Macbook, there are a few other essential tools I use when designing. They include:

  • Wacom Tablet
  • Mouse
  • Canon DSLR

I don't know what I would do without my Wacom Tablet. It makes everything so much easier! As far as software goes, I mostly use Adobe's Creative Suite. Recently since I have a Digital sculpting class, I am also experimenting with Zbrush.

I find that it's easier to work with music or the TV going in the background. For some it can be a distraction, but I think that it gets my "creative juices" flowing.
